Sort the numbers from small to large according to the rules below step by step, beginning with the first rule.
First, sort the numbers by their last digit, numbers with a higher last digit are larger.
Second, for numbers with the same first digit, numbers with more odd digits are larger.
Third, for numbers with the same number of odd digits, numbers with a larger sum of its digits are larger.
Fourth, for numbers with the same sum of its digits, numbers with more digits are larger.
Fifth, for numbers with the same number of digits, sort them from small to large normally.

Please arrange the boxes in order from left to right.
